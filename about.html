About Us

We Are The World's Largest Workshop Firm

Workshop Inc. is a multi-disciplinary design practice comprising of architects, designers and artists operating within the fields of architecture, interior design, products, graphics and installations. Based in Ahmedabad, the practice takes a consistent, holistic approach to spatial design and interior architecture and offers a profound understanding and interpretation of the full range of creative possibilities.

We seek out narratives for each project and focus less on form–making. Our projects are developed from a core idea that governs the design, using simple solutions to achieve the end-product. The resultant of our cross-disciplinary work allows us to fuse art, graphics and space as a part of a single canvas.
